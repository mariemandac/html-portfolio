Best Movies of All Time According to Marie

My Top 3 Movies

Edge of Tomorrow

Action, Sci-fi. The movie skillfully blends elements of science fiction, action, and even a touch of comedy, creating a captivating narrative that keeps viewers engaged throughout. The plot is well-paced, with a perfect balance of intense combat sequences, character-driven moments, and intriguing twists.

Guardians of the Galaxy Volume 3

Adventure, Sci-Fi. I love Rocket, Lyla, Teefs and Floor!

The Wailing

Horror. The movie revolves around a small village gripped by a mysterious illness and a series of gruesome deaths.
